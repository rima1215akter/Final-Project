Project Manager (Operations)

ASL BPO

Key Selling Points

  • Must have 5+ years of experience in Managerial position / Project management preferably in a well-reputed outsourcing company
  • Must be Fluent In English Communication in Both Written and Verbal
  • PMP Certification Preferred

Vacancy

1

Job Context

  • ASL BPO is a BPO / Outsourcing Firm that provides outsourcing solutions to international businesses and global brands abroad. We do this through a collaborative and consultative approach, rethinking business processes and ensuring our clients get the outsourcing solutions they need. We`re an ambitious team of professionals looking to disrupt our industry by delivering seamless customer experiences for our clients, backed-up with exceptional results.
  • As a Project Manager, you will have the opportunity to manage and handle one of our inbound customer care projects for one of our Hungarian clients consisting of 50+ team members. You will be planning, overseeing, and leading the team as well as communicating with the client to ensure the success of the project.

Job Responsibilities

  • This will be 12 PM Noon to 9 PM 6 days a week job
  • Supervise and Manage an Inbound customer care project with 50+ team members for a Hungarian client
  • Ensure the team is working 100% efficiently and is reaching client-required SLA & QA rating
  • Ensure the team has 95% attendance as required by the client.
  • Communicate with clients and resolve all their needs
  • Keep track of work efficiency metrics for all team members to find out the best performers, as well as those who are lagging
  • Create and plan steps to ensure improvement of those employees who cannot reach client-required SLA & QA rating
  • Create and plan steps to ensure new recruits for the projects are brought up to speed to meet client-required SLA & QA ratings.
  • Resolve any employee-related issues of the project.

Employment Status

Full-time

Workplace

  • Work at office

Educational Requirements

  • Master of Business Management (MBM), Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Masters in Business Administration or Masters in Business Management from any well-reputed university such as NSU, BRAC, or any other.
  • PMP certification will be highly preferred
  • Preferred Professional Certification: PMP Certification

Experience Requirements

  • At most 4 year(s)
  • The applicants should have experience in the following area(s): Administrative Management, Project Management
  • The applicants should have experience in the following business area(s):
  • Call Center, IT Enabled Service

Additional Requirements

  • Age at least 35 years
  • Only males are allowed to apply
  • Skills Required: Good communication skills, Active listening, Compassionate, Proactive, Committed, Flexible, Accountability, Solution-oriented, Detail-oriented, Action-oriented
  • Proven working experience in handling projects and teams
  • Must be willing to work long term with our company
  • Fluent in English speaking is a MUST

Job Location

Dhaka(Uttara)

Salary

    Negotiable

  • Salary Review: Yearly
  • Festival Bonus: 2
